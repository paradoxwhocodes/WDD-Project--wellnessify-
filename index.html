Cardiovascular Equipment

These machines allow you to walk, jog, or run indoors on a moving surface. They typically have adjustable speeds and incline levels, making them a versatile option for a variety of fitness levels.

Strength Training Equipment

Rowing machines work your entire body and provide a low-impact cardiovascular workout. They simulate the motion of rowing a boat, and can be a great way to build strength and endurance.

Group Fitness Class

This class will include training such as high-intensity interval training (HIIT), yoga, or dance. It's a great opportunity to get some exercise, meet new people, and have fun!
